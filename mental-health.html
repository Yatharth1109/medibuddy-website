Yoga for Mental Health

Daily relaxing exercises you can follow for better emotional and mental well-being.

1. Anulom Vilom

Breathe in and out through alternate nostrils. Calms the nervous system and promotes focus.

2. Child's Pose

Relieves tension from the back and shoulders. Helps with anxiety and deep relaxation.

3. Deep Breathing

Simple inhale-exhale for 5 minutes. Reduces stress hormones and balances mood.
